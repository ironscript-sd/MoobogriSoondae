Our Story

The Art of
the Broth.

At Moobongri Irvine, we believe a great bowl of soup is a labor of love. Our signature Soondae Guk begins with a broth simmered for hours to achieve a milky, deep complexity that stays true to our roots.

Whether you're here for our world-class blood sausage or our sizzling hot pots, every dish is a tribute to the flavors of Korea, reimagined for the Irvine community. We don't just serve food; we serve memories of home.
